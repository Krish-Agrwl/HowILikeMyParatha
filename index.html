Aloo Paratha Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups whole wheat flour
  • 3 medium potatoes, boiled and mashed
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 2 green chilies, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons coriander leaves, chopped
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil or ghee for cooking

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix the flour with water to make a soft dough.
  2. In another bowl, mix mashed potatoes with cumin, chilies, coriander, and salt.
  3. Divide the dough and filling into equal portions.
  4. Roll out the dough, place filling in the center, and seal it.
  5. Roll out again and cook on a hot griddle with oil or ghee until golden brown.

Paneer Paratha Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups whole wheat flour
  • 200g paneer, grated
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 tablespoon green chilies, chopped
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil or ghee for cooking

Instructions

  1. Mix flour with water to make a soft dough.
  2. Combine grated paneer with garam masala, chilies, and salt.
  3. Divide the dough and filling into equal portions.
  4. Roll out the dough, place filling in the center, and seal it.
  5. Cook on a hot griddle with oil or ghee until golden brown.

Gobi Paratha Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups whole wheat flour
  • 1 cup cauliflower, grated
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 tablespoon green chilies, chopped
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil or ghee for cooking

Instructions

  1. Prepare the dough by mixing flour with water until soft.
  2. Mix grated cauliflower with cumin, chilies, and salt.
  3. Divide the dough and filling into equal portions.
  4. Roll out the dough, add filling, seal it, and roll out again.
  5. Cook on a hot griddle with oil or ghee until golden brown.
